There are four Harry Potter houses. They are:

  • Gryffindor
  • Ravenclaw
  • Hufflepuff
  • Slytherin

Each house is defined by their characteristic trait.

  • Gryffindors are brave
  • Ravenclaws are smart
  • Hufflepuffs are kind
  • Slytherins are ambitious

Students are sorted into their houses by the sorting hat.
